Highlights

Tokyo office opened Feb 3. First three deals closed (Rakuten, SoftBank, MUFG). $1.2M TCV in 7 weeks.

Watch Items
SMB churn ticked up
Logo churn rose to 4.2% (from 3.1%). Root cause: pricing sensitivity in sub-$10K cohort. Mitigation plan in place — launching starter tier in April.
Engineering hiring behind
Filled 6 of 12 planned roles. Senior backend remains competitive. Adjusted comp bands and added referral bonus ($10K → $15K).
Infrastructure costs rising
Cloud spend grew 32% QoQ vs. 24% revenue growth. Migration to reserved instances underway — expect 20% savings by Q3.
